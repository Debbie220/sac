Controlling Presentations

  • Click on the Presentations tab to view all of the current presentations.
  • Click on a presentation's title to see more details about that presentation.
  • To edit a presentation, click the button.
  • The indicates whether or not a presentation has been nominated for the Outstanding Undergraduate Research award.
  • The status of each presentation is shown on the right hand side of the page. Saved means the user has not yet submitted the presentation. Pending Approval means the user has submitted the presentation, but the admin has not yet approved it. Approved means that the presentation is approved, and is ready for scheduling. Declined means that there was something wrong with the presentation, and the admin has declined it. In this case, the user must make whatever changes are necessary, re-submit it.
  • To view all presentations that are Pending Approval and approve/decline them, click in the top right corner of the page.

Creating a new Conference

Before students are able to register for their presentations, you must create a new conference. First click on the Rooms tabd and add the rooms that will be needed for the conference Only rooms marked as will be added to the conference. NOTE: Do not create a new conference before the current one has ended.

  1. Click on Create a new conference on the side menu
  2. Type in the conference name
  3. If the conference is for more than one day then click to add a new day to the conference
  4. Input the start times for the first time slot and the start times for the last time slots for each Day

    NOTE: the time format is 24 hours

Scheduling

  • Click on the Schedule link to view the scheduling page.
  • The student names, titles, and OUR statuses of all of the approved presentations will appear in the well on the left hand side of the page.
  • The rooms that are available in the current conference are shown along the top of the page. Click on a room to view the timeslots available for that room.
  • The available timeslots will be shown on the right hand side of the page, ordered by day. To assign a presentation to a certain timeslot, drag the presentation into that timeslot. Be sure to press before leaving the page or moving to another room.
  • To unschedule a presentation, simply drag it back to the well on the left hand side of the page.
  • To add a timeslot, press at the bottom right of the page. Specify the day, the starting hour, and the starting minute of the new timeslot and hit
