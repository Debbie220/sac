Professors

Professors can do anything a student can. They can also see, edit and delete any presentation under one of their courses. Remember that before you can do all that, you must request a professor role in the systems.

If you are looking for other roles, check the home page

Request Professor Role

If you are a professor at Augustana, you need to request this role after your signup! For security issues, every user is registered as student.

To do that, just click on 'Request Professor Role' on your navigation bar. If your request is approved, you will have access to the professor home page next time you login.
