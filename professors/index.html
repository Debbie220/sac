Professors

Professors can do anything a student can. They can also see, edit and delete any presentation under one of their courses. Remember that, before any of that, you must request a professor role in the system.

If you are looking for other roles, check the home page


Request Professor Role

If you are a professor at Augustana, you need to request this role after your signup! For security issues, every user is registered as student.

To do that, just click on 'Request Professor Role' on your navigation bar. If your request is approved, you will have access to the professor home page next time you login.


Add courses to your profile

We can't access the courses you teach automatically yet and that's why you have to add the courses you teach to your profile. Once you do that, you'll be able to see (edit and delete) any presentation under that course.

  1. Click on ' My Courses'
  2. Select the course you want to add.
  3. Press 'Add Course'

Remove courses from your profile

For the same reason outlined in the previous topic, you also have to manually remove a course from you profile when the semester is done (or at the beginning of the next one). You won't be prompted to confirm this action, but you can just readd the course if needed.

This action doesn't delete the course. It only removes from your profile.
