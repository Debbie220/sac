Students

Students can add, edit, submit and delete presentations for SAC. These can be for a whole group or just for themselves!

Create Presentation

  1. Click on ' Add Presentation'
  2. Add the name of your surpevisor for this presentation
  3. Your name will appear in 'Student Name'. You don't have to type anything there unless it's a group presentation. In this case, select 'Group' and add the name of your fellow student. If you need to add more than one, just click on ' Add Student' and type the new name on the empty input field.
  4. Select the course this presentation refers to (the major one in case of multiple).
  5. Add the title of your presentation
  6. Choose the type of it (if you don't know the right one, check with your professor!)
  7. If you already have an abstract ready, just type it in. If you don't, don't worry for now, because it's not required until a little later. The SAC Coodinator will let your professor know the deadline for this.
  8. If the SAC Coordinator needs to know anything about your presentation or your schedule, or any special needs, let them know by typing it in 'Special Notes'
  9. Press ' Save'
